Who We Are

About PIOCCI

People of Indian Origin Chamber of Commerce and Industry (PIOCCI) fosters collaboration and networking opportunities among people of Indian origin and businesses. With a diverse membership base, including entrepreneurs, industry leaders, and professionals from various sectors, PIOCCI strives to enhance trade relations, facilitate investments, advocate and influence policies.

Our professionals connect you with potential business partners, generate leads, and provide customised professional services. PIOCCI helps you grow your network, find new clients, and navigate the global market.

PIOCCI was registered as a national society in India on October 6, 2003. We have a full-time secretariat in Delhi that oversees all activities in India. PIOCCI International delivers services worldwide through registered entities in select geographies and directly in countries where PIOCCI does not have entities.

We have spawned chapters such as PIOCCI USA, PIOCCI Canada, and PIOCCI Limited in Dubai, registered in respective geographies. Each organisation has its team that serves the region.

Our Distinction

Why Us?

Indian diaspora has many organizations in all parts of the world. However, these associations are primarily local or national. They do not have a footprint in India or other regions of the world. PIOCCI seeks to bridge that gap.

We are a Global Business Platform for Indian Diaspora and have a presence and activities in more than 50 countries spanning all parts of the globe. And we also have chapters in all Indian states.

Therefore, we are a bridge for the Indian diaspora to do business in India and worldwide. We collaborate and complement diaspora organisations, bilateral, and other business chambers.

"A bridge for the Indian diaspora to do business in India and worldwide."
